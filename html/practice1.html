Mahatma Gandhi

The Indian Activist


Gandhi was the leader of India's non-violent independence movement against British rule.
Also, in South Africa, he fought for the civil rights of Indians, who were discriminated greatly.
He was killed by a fanatic in 1948.

Mahatma Gandhi
  • Fought against racial discrimination in South Africa
  • Successfully led a non-violent tax revolt in Kheda
  • Led the famous Salt March to Dandi
  • Launched the Quit India Movement in 1942 to end British rule
  1. Was TIME's Person of the Year
  2. Fought against social evils such as the Untouchables
  3. Nominated for Noble Peace Prize five times
  4. Walked 18 km a day because he believed walking was the best exercise
