Foods that I eat day to day

These photos are the foods that I eat a lot; Hover over them!

This dish is called pho and is eaten almost everywhere in Vietnam. It's a beef noodle soup with meat in it; people usually put herbs, cilantro, onions, and jalapenos to make things taste better. My mom probably makes this once every 3 weeks or so and it tastes really good and can even be pretty healthy depending on how many herbs you put on.
This dish is called bun bo hue and is common throughout Vietnam. It is a spicy beef noodle soup(different from pho though) with a bunch of toppings and meat on top. My mom makes this about once or twice a month and I mostly enjoy eating it because it tastes good and makes me full. Sometimes I get sick of it because I eat it too much.
I pretty much eat Digorno's pizza once every month because I crave pizza very often. I buy a 3 pack from Sam's Club. I choose the Supreme version because it has a lot of veggies with sausages and pepperonis. I usually throw away the crust because it's too hard for my teeth and I don't want to damage my teeth, especially after I had my braces off.
Last but not least, I eat rice a lot because I'm asian. I don't eat it plain although I see some people eat it like that, which is weird. I usually eat it with some sort of meat and vegetable. I eat rice whenever I'm not eating these noodle soups and usually eat it when I have a lot of homework and need to eat fast.
